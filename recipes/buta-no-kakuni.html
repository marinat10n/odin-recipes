Instant Pot Pork Belly (Buta no Kakuni) Recipe

Description:

Instant pot pork belly recipe based off Just One Cookbook recipe. Estimated time to completion is 1 hr 10 mins.

Ingredients:

  • 3 green onions/scallions
  • 1 knob ginger peeled and sliced
  • 2 lb pork belly block w/o rind (*note: 3-4lb requires 50% increase in seasonings)
  • daikon
  • 4 soft-boiled eggs
  • shichimi togarashi
For Seasonings:
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup sake
  • 1/2 cup mirin
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup sugar (4 Tbsp)

Directions:

  1. Gather ingredients. Cut pork belly into 5cm x 5 cm cubes.
  2. Cut scallions (long slices) and ginger.
  3. Using Saute setting on Instant Pot. Heat 1 Tbsp oil. Add pork belly in single layers and sear both sides. May need to be done in batches.
  4. When finished, add in all of the pork belly into the instant pot. Pour in enough water to cover the meet. Add green onions and ginger. Set Instant Pot to manual and cook for 35 minutes.
  5. Vent steam or natural release (~15 min). Drain water, onion, ginger. Rinse pork belly under warm water.
  6. Plave pork belly back in Instant Pot. Add and mix 1/2 cup water, 1/4 cup sake, 1/2 cup miring, 1/2 cup soy sauce, 1/4 cup sugar. Add peeled eggs. Press Saute. Bring to simmer. Switch to Manual 10 min setting for sliced pork belly or 20 min for cubed pork belly.
  7. Optional: Vent the steam. Remove lid. Saute on low until liquid reduces by half.
