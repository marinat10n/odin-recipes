Cookies: Oatmeal Chocolate Chip

Description:

Recipe sourced from the "Chew Chocolate Chip Oatmeal Cookies" allrecipes site. Small adjustments made based on reviews, trial and error, and to accomodate dietary restrictions (dairy, gluten).

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup coconut oil, softened
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs (room temp)
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 3/4 cups almond flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups old-fashioned (or quick-cooking) oats
  • 1 cup chopped & toasted walnuts
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
