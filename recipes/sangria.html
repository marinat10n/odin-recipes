Sangria "Recipe"

Description:

Recipe sourced from an Airbnb host, Mercedes, in Barcelona, Spain (2016).

Ingredients:

The Foundation:

  • 3 Oranges juiced
  • 1 Lemon juiced
  • Red wine
  • 3 spoons brown sugar

Supporting Cast:

  • 1 Apple sliced
  • 1 Orange sliced
  • 2 Kiwis sliced
  • A few dates

The Main Characters:

  • Brandy 7 sec pour
  • Vermouth 5 sec pour (generous finish)
  • Apple Liquor 7 sec pour
  • Cinnamon stick
  • 1 spoon cinammon powder
  • White wine to top it off

Directions:

Establish the foundation. Add in the supporting cast. Bring on the main characters.
